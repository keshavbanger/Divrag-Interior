Lighting is the secret ingredient that defines the mood, highlights architecture, and ensures functionality in any interior space. At Divrag Interior, we approach lighting design through layering—combining ambient, task, and accent lighting to create a rich, multi-dimensional environment.

We select fixtures that range from sculptural statement pieces to discreet architectural lighting. Our expertise also includes smart lighting controls and energy-efficient LED integrations that allow you to change the atmosphere of a room with a single touch.

  • Layered Lighting (Ambient, Task, Accent)
  • Architectural Spotlighting & Uplighting
  • Smart Home Automated Lighting Controls
  • Energy Efficient LED Solutions
  • Decorative Statement Fixtures
  • Natural Light Integration & Optimization
