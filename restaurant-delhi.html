Delhi Gourmet Hub

Home / Projects / Restaurant & Café
  • Project Date: 05 May, 2024
  • Project Duration: 6 Months
  • Category: Hospitality Design
  • Client / Company: Azure Flavors
  • Project Location: New Delhi, India

The design concept

We fused industrial aesthetics with warm, organic elements to create a space that transitions seamlessly from a daytime cafe to an upscale evening restaurant. The use of reclaimed wood and exposed brick adds character and depth.

Key features

  • Custom-designed open kitchen viewing gallery.
  • Sound-absorbing ceiling installations.
  • Biophilic walls integrated into the seating areas.
  • Optimized lighting for food photography.
